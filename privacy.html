Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Introduction

OnlineGridMaker ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website at onlinegridmaker.com (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our Service.

How We Handle Your Data

OnlineGridMaker is designed so your images stay on your device. When you use the Service:

  • Your images are processed entirely in your browser using JavaScript and the Canvas API.
  • Images are never uploaded to any server.
  • We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use the site and improve the Service.
  • We do not require account creation, email addresses, or any form of identification.

Browser-Only Processing

All image processing happens locally on your device. The image file is read by your browser using the FileReader API, drawn onto an HTML5 Canvas element with grid overlays applied, and then exported as a PNG download. At no point does the image leave your device.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Analytics 4 to measure site usage. Google Analytics may collect information such as user counts, session statistics, approximate geolocation, and browser or device information, and may use first-party cookies to distinguish users and sessions.

If we deploy the Service through a content delivery network (such as Cloudflare), that provider may log basic access logs (IP address, user agent, request timestamp) for security and operational purposes. These logs are governed by the CDN provider's own privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us
